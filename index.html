About Free Cage

FUTURE SCENARIO

Technologies like the Internet of Things, artificial intelligence and augmented reality prefigure a future in which the identity of objects, as well as their mutual interaction and control, will be increasingly fragmented between the real and the virtual.

In the future every object will be less and less the single material entity and more and more the system of functions and interactions that it will develop with all the other objects that surround it.

Today we use tablet and mobile to access the world of information, but tomorrow every object will be a door to access a virtual dimension with which it will interact; furthermore, each object will be potentially usable as a tool for automatically interacting with other objects, collecting data, exchanging them reciprocally and automatically creating interactions and actions with things, people and ecosystems. Each object will modify the other objects according to its parameters and the variation of its characteristics will automatically change the physical state of the other interconnected objects.

Every real object will be like the knot of a great net that will envelop us really and virtually and the matter of things will be only a condensed point of a great vibration of things, sounds, lights, data, materials, words...

And this great network will immediately show itself in its twofold great and critical aspect: it will be freedom and it will be imprisonment.

FREE CAGE

FREEDOM OR IMPRISONMENT? A PROJECT BETWEEN REALITY AND VIRTUALITY

Free cage is a project that aims to represent the ambiguity of the current and future condition described in the aforementioned “Future Scenario”.

It does not want to give answers but only to stage the questions and doubts in front of the observer.

The oxymoron "free cage" wants to represent the duplicity of our dependendant relationship with the network of objects that surrounds us, which will be more and more interconnected with our life. We will possess and be possessed by objects. More and more.

We will be their masters and we will be their slaves, it will depend only on the point of view.

We, sailors and prisoners, will be increasingly caught up in this vast network of tangible and intangible entities. We will be more and more inside a prison with open cages, and we will be like those birds that no longer flee from their cage even the doors are open.

We will be at the same time both the wolf and the dog of Aesop's fable, without knowing which of the two identities we will be interpreting with our choices.

Where is freedom? Where is the prison? Where is the matter? Where is transcendence? Which song will we sing? The power or frustration? The escape or the return?

Free cage is an installation that, like the objects of the future, develops itself between two worlds in reciproc relation: the “material world”,that we call REALITY, inhabited by our bodies, and the “computational world”, that we call VIRTUALITY, increasingly inhabited by our perception.
